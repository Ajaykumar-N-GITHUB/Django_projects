📘 Read Me - Farm Management System

  • After submitting an audio file, please allow a few seconds for processing, as the AI works internally to analyze and save the data.
  • A login alert will be sent for every successful login.
  • Workers can take their own time to record audio. Recording will continue until they manually press the stop button.
  • Owners are encouraged to use the application for a few days to get familiar with its features and train their workers accordingly.
  • 💡 For best results, ensure a stable internet connection when uploading audio or generating reports.
  • If a worker’s audio submission results in an error, they should try rephrasing the message in a different way with the same meaning.
  • Only one type of entry can be submitted at a time — either an investment or a harvest, but not both together.
  • While recording audio, make sure to clearly mention the item name, the activity type (buy or sold), and the amount.

✨ Key Features

  • 👨‍🌾 Separate dashboards are available for owners, managers, and workers for a user-friendly experience.
  • 🎙️ Workers can record short voice messages (Tamil/English) to log investment or harvest details via the left-side panel.
  • 📝 Workers can submit detailed daily work reports (Hindi/Tamil/English) through the right-side panel.
  • ✅ Worker submissions will first appear in the manager’s dashboard. Only after manager approval will they be displayed in the owner’s dashboard.
  • 🔍 Managers can review and approve/reject worker submissions from their dashboard.
  • 👨‍🌾 Owners can edit or remove the entries once records are approved by managers.
  • 📋 Owners can view the complete list of managers and workers by clicking the Manage button in the top-right corner of the dashboard.
  • ➕➖ Owners can add or remove workers from the owner dashboard.
  • 📊 Owners can download expense reports on a monthly or yearly basis.
  • 📑 Owners can download worker log reports for a day, week, month, or year.
  • ⏰ Owners can set reminders for important farm activities.
  • ✏️ Owners can edit or remove incorrect investment/harvest entries made by workers.
  • 📱 Since this is a web app, there may be minor UI mismatches on mobile devices (actively being improved).
